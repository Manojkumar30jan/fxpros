Autonomous Product Scan

ImageScan is an autonomous image and video scanning technology that can
identify products from bar codes, QR codes, dimensions and information painted
on the boxes or outside the products.

It is currently used to scan products in receivers with conveyer belts in
warehouses and retail stores. It will also be used to identify state of inventory in
the warehouses and stores.

ImageScan has significant improved performance as compared to bar code
scanners or line scanners.
